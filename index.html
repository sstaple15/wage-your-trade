Wage Your Trade War

Trade wars and the tariffs they bring are far more than national distraction - they are real wars on household jobs and industries. Using data from the Brookings Institute, this tool estimates the number of jobs made vulnerable to loss by tariffs. Explore tariff rates - starting with those in March 2019 - to see who loses out from protectionist policies, down to your own county. Wage your own trade war to see which states get the most from trade.

    Source: Personally consolidated estimates using data from the National Export Survey (Operated by the US Census Bureau) and metrics from the Brookings Institute.

    Methodology: We estimate trade exposure as a percentage of total area employment by consolidating county- and industry-specific estimates from the Brookings Institute
    over years 2012-2018. This is then crossed with total exports per industry to create pseudo-supply curves. From these, the impact of trade tariffs is linearly estimated. This
    process does allow some instances when total jobs impacted may exceed 100% - this is due to firm over-reporting of employment for headquarter locations.
